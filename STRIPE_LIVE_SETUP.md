# Stripe Live Setup Guide

By default, the ModelLink platform runs in Demo Mode (`MARKETPLACE_DEMO=true`), which provides a mocked, 100% reliable checkout experience that does not require live Stripe webhooks.

If you are a developer looking to enable the **Real Stripe Payment Flow** for a live production deployment (like on a VM or Vercel/Render), follow these exact steps to ensure your backend can securely verify and process Stripe webhooks.

## Steps to Enable Live Stripe Webhooks

1. **Log into your Stripe Dashboard** in your browser.
2. Go to **Developers > Webhooks**.
3. Click **"Add an endpoint"**.
4. Enter the actual live URL of your backend (e.g., `https://api.your-domain.com/api/webhook` or `http://<YOUR_VM_IP>:8000/api/webhook`).
5. Select the events your backend listens for (specifically `checkout.session.completed` and `payment_intent.succeeded`).
6. Click **Add Endpoint**.
7. Stripe will now reveal a brand new **Signing Secret** (it starts with `whsec_...`).
8. Copy that exact secret, uncomment `STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET` in your server's `.env` file, and paste the new real secret there.
9. Ensure you have `MARKETPLACE_DEMO=false` and `REACT_APP_MARKETPLACE_DEMO=false` set in your respective `.env` files.

> **⚠️ CRITICAL:** Without this exact real secret, your live backend will reject all incoming Stripe payments as "invalid signature" to protect against hackers! Do not use local testing webhook secrets (like those generated by the `stripe listen` CLI) in a live environment.
